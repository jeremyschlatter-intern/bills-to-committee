How It Works

Data Collection

We analyzed thousands of bills from the 114th-118th Congresses (2015-2024) using the Congress.gov API, tracking which committees each bill was referred to.

Pattern Recognition

The model identifies patterns between bill characteristics (policy area, subjects, title keywords) and committee referrals. Policy area is the strongest predictor, as committees have defined jurisdictions.

Prediction

For new bills, the model combines signals from policy area, subject tags, and title analysis to predict the most likely committee(s). Multi-referral is supported for bills spanning multiple jurisdictions.
